Five Places That Shouldn’t Exist Together

The deeper you go, the less the forest feels like a single place.

  • One path leads into a bright meadow where everything feels alive and open.

  • Another pulls you into darker woods where shapes blur and the air feels heavier.

  • Then suddenly, you are somewhere underground — surrounded by crystals that reflect more than just light.

  • And above it all, a canopy stretches higher than it should, almost as if the forest is trying to grow beyond itself.

  • Ruins appear where nothing should remain

Every area has its own vibe and really changes how you approach the puzzles.
