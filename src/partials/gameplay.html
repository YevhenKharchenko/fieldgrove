It Feels Simple… Until It Isn’t

  • You begin with a few pieces. Apples, gems, small glowing elements scattered across a quiet board. You swap them. Match three. Then another.

  • Nothing complicated.

  • Then the board shifts.

  • You notice that not every move is equal. Some open space. Some close it. Some create opportunities you didn’t expect. You pause for a second longer before your next move.

  • That’s when the game really starts.
